Specs and Compatibility

Connector Types

  • DVI-D (Digital Only)
    • Single-Link: 18+1 pins
    • Dual-Link: 24+1 pins
  • DVI-A (Analog Only)
    • 17 pins (12+5 configuration)
  • DVI-I (Integrated Digital & Analog)
    • Single-Link: 23 pins (18+5)
    • Dual-Link: 29 pins (24+5)

Link Types

  • Single-Link
    • Bandwidth: Up to 3.96 Gbps
    • Max Resolution: 1920×1200 @ 60Hz
  • Dual-Link
    • Bandwidth: Up to 7.92 Gbps
    • Max Resolution: 2560×1600 @ 60Hz

Compatibility

  • DVI-D cables connect digital-only devices.
  • DVI-A cables connect analog-only devices.
  • DVI-I cables support both digital and analog signals, but not simultaneously.
  • Adapters are available for DVI to VGA (analog) and DVI to HDMI (digital) connections.

Additional Features

  • Supports hot-plugging and EDID for display identification.
  • Maximum recommended cable length:
    • Up to 4.5 meters for resolutions up to 1920×1200.
    • Longer lengths may require signal boosters.
